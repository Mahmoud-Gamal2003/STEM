Welcome To STEM World

Here I will tell you all things you went to know about STEM program in the College of Education.

Type of Study

The school subjects in the College of Education are divided into two types:

1

Educational Subjucts

These are the subjects concerned with teaching methods and learning theories. These subjects are what contribute to the preparation of your character as a teacher.

2

Specialization Subjucts

These are the subjects that provide the student with the information he needs in his specialization
Example
If your major is chemistry, you will learn in these subjects thermochemistry, electrical chemistry and organic chemistry...etc.

Features

After graduation, the student will be a (STEM) teacher, which is a rare specialty in Egypt and the Arab world.

The graduate will be able to teach in excelling schools, international schools, languages, private schools, and experimental schools.

Upon graduation, the student obtains a bachelor's degree from the College of Education, a college accredited by the National Authority for Quality Assurance of Education.

Common Questions

What is the deffrent between STEM program and other programs?

At STEM program you will be studying active learning strategies and collective action they qualify to be teacher in STEM School . Unlike the rest of the programs that focus on passive learning.

Is the STEM program graduate can only work in STEM school?

No, They can work in STEM schools, international schools, language schools and government schools.

What is the capstone project?

It is a project that is done every term on one of Egypt's great challenges. Students are divided into groups of 5 to 8 people. Each group should search for the best possible solution to this problem.
